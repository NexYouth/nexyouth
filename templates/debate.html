Meet the head coach

Why William leads the program

William Zhou

Head Coach · Upper Canada College

William was a natural choice to head the program. What first stood out was his critical thinking: the instinct to question and pressure-test ideas that defines every strong debater. As a teacher he keeps things simple, explaining concepts clearly without the jargon that can lose younger students.

He also brings real ownership, having built the full curriculum and lesson plans for all three levels himself. At Upper Canada College he served as a junior executive on the debate team, where he ran a mentorship programme for younger debaters. We're glad to have him on the NexYouth team.

Summer Debate Workshop, in person

A five-day, in-person debate workshop run with the Achēv Newcomer Information Centre, developing critical thinking, argumentation, and persuasive speaking.

The program

Over five days students cover debate structure, motion analysis, speaker roles, strategy, and formal debate practice, learning to present ideas logically, respond effectively, and communicate with confidence in structured discussions.
