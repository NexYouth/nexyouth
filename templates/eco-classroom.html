How EcoClassroom works

Every course follows the same structure. Pick a topic that interests you, work at your own pace, and earn a certificate when you finish.

1

Register

Sign up once with your email — your progress is saved across all courses.

2

Learn

Each lesson includes a reading, a video, and a short reflection question.

3

Complete

Pass the quizzes (60%+) and submit your final project.

4

Earn

Receive your NexYouth EcoHero Certificate plus a completion award.
