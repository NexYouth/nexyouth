How it works

The same simple path in every course

Pick a topic that interests you, work at your own pace, and earn a certificate when you finish.

1. Register

Sign up once with your email — your progress is saved across all courses.

2. Learn

Each lesson includes a reading, a video, and a short reflection question.

3. Complete

Pass the quizzes (60% or higher) and submit your final project.

4. Earn

Receive your NexYouth Eco Hero Certificate plus a completion award.
