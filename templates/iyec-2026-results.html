Eutrophication silently suffocates freshwater ecosystems, turning lakes into dead-zones. Project SANA gives these waters a voice. City Air Purification Technology in Almaty: Converting Smog into Energy
A groundbreaking concept treating Almaty's toxic winter smog as a resource rather than waste.
