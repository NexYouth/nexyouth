About NexYouth Society

A youth-led nonprofit for education equity

NexYouth Society is a youth-led nonprofit organisation focused on education access equity for youth. Through free and low-cost online and in-person programs, NexYouth provides mentoring, mentorship, and skill-building opportunities in areas such as math, science, coding, debate, language learning, and sustainability.

Accessible by design

Our programs are designed to be accessible, student-centered, and impact-driven, empowering youth not only to learn, but also to apply their knowledge to real-world challenges.
