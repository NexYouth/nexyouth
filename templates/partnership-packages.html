Our Programs & Global Reach

In addition to ongoing mentoring and educational courses, NexYouth organizes global youth initiatives and events that encourage students to think critically and act responsibly.

One of NexYouth's flagship initiatives is the International Youth Environmental Challenge (IYEC) — a global youth competition that attracts youth globally to tackle environmental and sustainability issues through research, advocacy, and problem-solving.

Together, NexYouth's programs and events create:

  • Continuous academic support
  • Leadership and skill development
  • Global collaboration opportunities for youth

About NexYouth Society

NexYouth Society is a youth-led nonprofit organization focused on education access equity for youth. Through free and low-cost online and in-person programs, NexYouth provides mentoring, mentorship, and skill-building opportunities in areas such as math, science, coding, debate, language learning, and sustainability.

Our programs are designed to be accessible, student-centered, and impact-driven, empowering youth not only to learn, but also to apply their knowledge to real-world challenges.
